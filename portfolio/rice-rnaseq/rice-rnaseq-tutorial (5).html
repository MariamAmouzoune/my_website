Case Study 01

Rice Transcriptome under M. oryzae Infection

A full reproduction of Cui et al. (2021) using WSL2, HISAT2, and DESeq2.

01. Workflow Overview

This project analyzes the transcriptomic reprogramming of rice in response to blast disease. We used 8 paired-end libraries (6 treated, 2 mock controls).

Bash — Step 1: Mapping (HISAT2)
for sample in control1 Treated1R1; do
  hisat2 --dta-cufflinks -p 6 -x rice_index \
         -1 ${sample}_1.fastq -2 ${sample}_2.fastq \
         | samtools sort -o sorted_${sample}.bam
done

02. Statistical Analysis in R

R — Differential Expression
library(DESeq2)
dds <- DESeqDataSetFromMatrix(countData = dat, colData = coldata, design = ~ condition)
dds <- DESeq(dds)
res <- results(dds, contrast=c("condition", "T", "C"))
Figure 1: Volcano plot showing upregulated (red) and downregulated (blue) genes in infected vs control samples.
